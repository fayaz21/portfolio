Philosophy

How I build.

Three principles that guide every project — from prototype to production.

01
🏗️

Systems Before Models

The best model in a broken system will fail. I design data flows, APIs, and infrastructure first — so the intelligence layer can iterate safely and quickly. Production readiness is a requirement, not an afterthought.

02
🔬

Evidence Over Intuition

Every design decision should be traceable to data or a clear constraint. I use experiments, evals, and measurement to choose between approaches — not hype or defaults. If I can't measure it, I instrument it.

03
🚀

Ship, Then Harden

I believe in fast feedback loops. Get a working v1 in front of real users, observe failure modes, then improve. Premature optimization is the enemy of learning. Build the thing that works, then build the thing that scales.
